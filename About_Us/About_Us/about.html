Our Journey

The Four Aces

In early 2021, four tech enthusiasts—Parv (product manager), Nandini (UI/UX designer), Om (back-end developer), and Neha (marketing strategist)—were on vacation when they encountered the frustration of booking various recreational activities on different platforms. This experience sparked their idea for a unified platform where users could easily browse, book, and manage their recreational activities from a single source.

Turning vision into reality

In early 2022, the team dove into development. Parv coordinated the project, ensuring everyone stayed on track and aligned with their goals. Nandini refined the design based on user feedback, while Om built the back-end infrastructure, creating a robust system for handling bookings and user data. Neha worked on pre-launch marketing, generating buzz through social media and engaging potential users.

Launch of ChillSlots

ChillSlots gets launched in August 2023, now one of the biggest game booking portals in the country. Early feedback was overwhelmingly positive. The team monitored performance, addressing any issues that arised and engaged with users on social media.

Market Expansion

Introduced advanced features, expanded into new markets, and optimized performance. The team is looking forward to future innovations.
